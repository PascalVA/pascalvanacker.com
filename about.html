# A little bit about myself

My name is Pascal. I am a tech enthusiast, as you can probably tell by my awesome website design. ;-) Born and raised in Belgium, I like all the good things that are typical of my country:
**French (??)** fries, beer, chocolate, waffles and for those who know me, a good pain saucisse.

I am currently working as a DevOps engineer at Tadaweb, an amazing company based in Luxembourg. They are always looking for amazing folks to join the team, so be sure to check them out at tadaweb.com.

As a DevOps engineer and SRE, I am, together with my team, responsible for the automation, monitoring and support of the it infrastructure that we provision for our clients on various bare-metal servers and clouds.
Besides that, we also take care of our CI/CD pipelines and work together with developers to increase productivity and efficiency.

Before that, I spent about five years working as a Linux system administrator at an SMO based in Leuven. I was managing and automating the Linux infrastructure and networking while also providing the build infrastructure for a CI/CD pipeline. On top of that my days were mixed with a hint of windows client and server systems.

In my spare time, I like to mess with computers, play some video games, eat and spend time with my friends and family. I've also been known to like a dad joke or two.
